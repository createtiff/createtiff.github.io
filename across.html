Making relocation easier by gathering local apps in one place




Idea/Question

How can we make relocation easier?

Inspiration

Inconvenience while relocating across countries and cities, such as having to do research, adapt, and switch to new systems in order to continue one's way of life.

Outcome

A mobile platform that converts applications across different cities, localizing the utility apps from the user's origin city to the destination city. It serves as a switch for applications when relocating, providing a list of applications that delivers the same functionality but adjusted to the local environment.

User Persona

• Gen Zs and Millennials, between the ages of 15 to 40
• digital nomads
• resides in urban settings
• Has to relocate across countries and cities due to work or education
