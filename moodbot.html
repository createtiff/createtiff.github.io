How might we integrate qualities unique to
 humans into our digital communication?

Emotions and facial expressions are fundamental to human communication. As technology gets deeply integrated into our lifestyle, the time spent facing screens increases while the time spent with humans' facial expressions decreases.

This project explores methods of reinterpreting human emotions and moods on digital communication, experimentation on the digital representation of facial expressions, bringing human qualities to our interaction with technology.
