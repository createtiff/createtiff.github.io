Dduk Guk
Korean Rice Cake Soup
떡국, Dduk Guk, a Korean traditional dish, is a meaningful heritage food for me. In Korean culture, we often eat Dduk Guk to celebrate Lunar New Year. My grandmother often cooked Dduk Guk for our family for speacial events. Needless to say, Dduk guk is a warm dish that fills up my heart and body, but also a powerful dish that is well balanced with nutrients.
