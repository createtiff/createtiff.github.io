Chanjoo Tiffany Park

                        is a Junior at Parsons School of Design studying BFA Communication Design and Creative Entrepreneurship. She was born in the suburbs of Chicago, raised in Seoul, South Korea, and moved to New York in 2016 to pursue her dream of being a fashion designer. After studying BFA fashion design at Parsons for two years she decided to change her path to communication design and entrepreneurship, which inspired her even more than fashion. Her current passion is in creating human centric visual systems, empathic design that focuses on the essentials, and bridging between physicality and digital age.
