Chanjoo Tiffany Park is a designer based in New York studying  BFA Communication Design with minors in Creative Entrepreneurship and Data Visualization at Parsons School of Design. She was born in the suburbs of Chicago, raised in Seoul, South Korea, and lived in Paris. Her passion is in bridging physical and digital spaces via crafting human centric visual systems that focuses on the essentials.
