Moodbot

Creative Coding

Project type

Individual/Class Project

Project timeframe

2 months, 2019 Fall

Skills

Creative Direction, Visual Design, Code

Technology used

HTML, CSS, Javascript

Reinterpreting emotional states and facial expressions of humans on digital screen through code




Question

How might we bring qualities that are unique to human to digital communication?

Inspiration

Emotions and facial expressions are key to human communication. As technology is being deeply integrated to our lifestyle, the time spent facing facial expressions of humans are decreasing while the time spent facing digital screens are increasing.

Goal

This project was aimed to explore methods of reinterpreting human emotion and mood on digital communication. Experimentation on digital representation of facial expressions, bringing human qualities in our interaction with technology.
