Weather Now

Mobile, Motion

Project type

Individual Project

Project timeframe

1 month, 2019 Fall + 1 month, 2020 Fall

Skills

Ideation, Wireframing, Prototype, Visual Design

Software used

Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

Supporting digital wellbeing by bringing natural ambiance driven by the sky and weather to mobile weather application




Idea/Question

How can we support digital wellbeing by creating ease in people’s daily use with technology?

Inspiration

As technology becomes more integrated into people’s daily lives, it is easy to be vulnerable to mental strain. It is more evident in urban settings such as New York City due to lack of breather in the environment of the city.

Outcome

A weather app that is designed to support digital wellbeing by bringing nature driven ambience to the screen, calming people’s minds and uplifting people’s mental strain.

User Persona

• Gen Zs, between the ages of 15 to 25 • Resides in the East Coast, New York City • People who struggle with mental strain due to high stress or environmental causes such as Seasonal Affective Disorder
