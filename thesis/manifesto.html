Interface Optimization
Manifesto

2020

More than ever in history, we live in a world where we make most of our communications through an interface.
Design

Design stands between humans and any non human entity as an interface.
Communication

Communication from one entity to another gets transmitted through a device despite the form being written, verbal, or visual.
Interface

Interface should be created not only for effective user experience but also to minimize digital burnout.
Optimize

Optimize interface design by considering visual perception in using digital devices.
Delivery

Deliver information into visual forms that make information digestible, feasible and pleasing to the eye. Take eye movement into consideration as well as the hierarchy and flow of information across the medium.
Clarity

Declutter by organizing and streamlining unnecessary noises that obstruct clear delivery of communication, meaning, and intention.
Experience

Convey the content by designing well-thought-out visual experiences to bring valuable engagement from the users.
Balance

Balance between designing the right amount of engagement that will interest the viewers and the state of void to prevent causing agitation.
