Weather Now

Mobile, Motion

How might we support digital wellbeing
by creating ease in people’s daily use
with technology?

Inspiration

As technology becomes more integrated into people’s daily lives, it is easy to be vulnerable to mental strain. It is more evident in urban settings such as New York City due to lack of breather in the environment of the city.

Solution

A weather app that is designed to support digital wellbeing by bringing nature driven ambience to the screen, calming people’s minds and uplifting people’s mental strain.

Target Users

People residing New York City, between the ages of 15 to 25, who struggle with mental strain due to high stress or environmental causes such as Seasonal Affective Disorder.
